About Me

Published: June 29, 2025 | Featured Post

Hi, I'm Praveen Yadav, a passionate developer with a strong focus on Python, Flutter, and Firebase. Over the years, I have worked on a wide range of projects that reflect my love for building practical and efficient software.

In Python, I've developed several real-world applications, including a Transport Management System, Student Management System, Dictionary App, QR Code Generator, and many more.

On the Flutter side, I specialize in building modern, responsive websites and mobile apps. Some of my notable projects include my personal Portfolio Website, the Minati Exchange, and a full-featured Gambling & Sports Betting Platform.

In 2024, I participated in a Hackathon where I developed Smart Tourism, a Flutter-based project designed to improve travel experiences using smart technology.

I believe in continuous learning, exploring new tools, and building software that solves real-world problems. Whether it's crafting user-friendly mobile apps or scalable web platforms, I enjoy turning ideas into reliable digital products.
